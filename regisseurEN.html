This series of posters is a graphic interpretation of three movies by director Stanley Kubrick, i.e. Killer's Kiss, Dr. Strangelove and The Killing. The violent nature of these movies was used as the basis for the graphic identity of the director.

A composition was made consisting of stills, the central murder weapon and three colors which can be linked to the concerned black and white movies.
